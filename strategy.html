About Boende
Boende.se has gathered data, indexed and published all of Sweden’s privately owned homes (3.5M),on the internet. Boende allow users to submit a bid on any home in Sweden whether it is for sale or not and allow homeowners to add a price they are willing to sell.

We spent five weeks analyzing Boende's:
– Business model
– KPI's and north star metric
– User journeys
– Google Analytics and Tag manager (looking into and altering)
– Hotjar (looking into and altering)
– UX challenges
– SEO (How to improve and optimize)
– Dashboard to meassure results on a regular basis

We delivered a report with insights from our analyses, and concrete actions Boende can take in order to improve how they work with their analytics tools. These insights will give Boende a better understanding of how to make most use of the tools.


Hyper Island Project: Data Landscape

About the project
This was the first project at Hyper Island – during 5 weeks, me and my team worked with the cloud collaboration platform Dropbox. We did research and analysed the business model and strategy, in order to come up with at least four hypotheses on how the company could grow, based on data and research.

We spent five weeks working with:
– Mapping the business model and strategy
– KPI's and target goals
– Customer journeys
– Growth hypotheses, and gathering data that support the hypotheses
– Tests to validate the hypotheses
– Estimating costs and gains of the the best growth hypothesis

"It is all word of mouth. Most of our business comes to us." – Dennis Woodside, COO at Dropbox

Growth Tactic
The hypothesis that we belived would have the biggest impact was a collaboration with Wework, where a free Dropbox account would be included in Wework's offer. This would increase awareness about the service and help Dropbox reach relevant target groups such as start-ups, freelancers and remote workers. This tactic would also support their overall communication strategy to spread awareness through word of mouth.

Role
Project Manager, and business development with the rest of the team
