Hyper Island Creative Task: SATS Social

Insights
Sitting still is one of our biggest health risks in western society. A lot of people don’t work out as much as they want to. Some people don’t work out enough to stay healthy.

https://www.svd.se/stillasittande-kan-vara-livsfarligt
https://www.svd.se/fetma-pa-vag-att-explodera-i-sverige

Why don’t people get up and move their bodies?
To some, working out seems to feel like a chore – a dull activity that needs to be done, just to lose weight or get a fit body. How can we change that mindset? – Some people seem to have found a key. Those people have in some way incorporated their social life in their workout routines.

“It is the togetherness with the others, and all the laughs that makes the training an unbeatable way of socializing” Susanne Hobohm, in interview for DN.

https://www.dn.se/sthlm/traning-ar-socialt-noje

How can we help people workout together?
The answer is SATS Social. SATS Social is a feature for the existing SATS ELIXIA app. The new feature enables friends to get notifications when friends book classes, socialize throughout their workout routines, and share tips, goals and favourite classes. Training is supposed to be fun!
