Resumé

My name is Mika Derkert and I am a Digital Strategist and Concept Developer with experience and knowledge within digital marketing, strategy, communication, strategic design, branding, and user experience.

I would say that my key strengths are digital strategy, branding, UX and concept development. I create everything from marketing- and growth strategies, to concepts and brand positioning strategies.

I have worked with marketing and communication for both B2C and B2B. And have experience from different industries, ranging from fashion to construction.

Through having experience from both design and digital strategy I have gotten a holistic view of marketing and communication, and have an understanding of different roles within the communication industry.

Key strengths:
– Digital Marketing and Strategy
– Brand positioning strategy
– Design and user experience
– Design processes and methods
– Presenting and pitching

When I'm not working, I like to excercise – I practice both yoga, cross training and kickboxing. I also love skiing and hiking which are hobbies I try to pursue at least a couple of times a year. Apart from kickboxing and being out in nature, I enjoy travelling and finding new music, movies, food, and so on.
